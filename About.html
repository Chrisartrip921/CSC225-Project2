Who Are We?

Our team at HYPEROOM is comprised of professional clothing buyers and sellers. We specialize in authenticating any and all brands of clothing to ensure our customers only buy authentic and quality clothing/sneakers.

What are we?

The objective for us over at HYPEROOM is to provide quality clothing; used or new. We are officially a consignment/retail store with one goal; to find the customers interests and match them up with their desirable clothing/sneakers.
